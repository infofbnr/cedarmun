About CedarMUN

CedarMUN is a new up-and-coming Model United Nations conference hosted in Lebanon. Our mission is to foster diplomacy, critical thinking, and global awareness among students from diverse backgrounds. At CedarMUN, participants engage in dynamic debates, collaborate on resolutions, and develop leadership skills in a supportive environment. Join us for an unforgettable experience where ideas take root and grow into meaningful action.
